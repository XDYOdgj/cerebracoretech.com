Frameless Bypass Shower Door

Elevate your bathroom with a sleek, modern frameless bypass shower door that blends style and functionality effortlessly.

Premium Material for Long-Lasting Performance

This frameless bypass shower door is crafted from high-quality 316 and 304 stainless steel, ensuring exceptional durability and a clean, minimalist design. The 3/8" thick tempered glass is double-certified for safety and features pre-drilled holes for hassle-free installation. The result is a flawless, frameless look that enhances the aesthetic of your bathroom space.

Perfect for Contemporary Bathrooms

With its matte black finish, this shower door complements modern fixtures and offers a sophisticated, high-end feel. The flexible left or right-side opening allows easy access from any direction, while the 4" horizontal adjustability makes it suitable for a wide range of bathroom layouts. Ideal for walk-in showers, it combines convenience with a whisper-quiet sliding system, minimizing noise and ensuring a smooth, rust-free finish.

Easy Installation & Complete Package

All necessary parts, hardware, and mounting accessories are included, making it simple to install without the need for extra purchases. The PVC sealant strip ensures a watertight seal, helping to prevent leaks and maintain a clean, dry environment in high-traffic bathrooms.

Comfort Meets Style

Experience the quiet sliding mechanism and the convenience of a one-finger pull, designed to make each use effortless and stress-free. The towel bar adds an extra touch of practicality and refinement. Whether you're upgrading your own bathroom or enhancing a commercial space, this door delivers both form and function with unmatched quality.
