API Pond Accu-Clear

API Pond Accu-Clear is a trusted water clarifier that helps eliminate dirt, silt, and floating particles from your pond. This powerful solution works quickly to restore clarity, making it easier for your pond filter to function at its optimal level. Regular use ensures a clean, healthy environment for your pond, making it a great choice for both new and established water gardens.

Whether you're dealing with cloudy water or want to maintain pristine conditions, API Pond Accu-Clear is the ideal addition to your pond care routine. It clumps particles together for effortless removal, improving the overall efficiency of your filtration system. With the ability to treat up to 9,600 US gallons of water, this pond treatment is perfect for large water gardens or ponds with heavy debris.
