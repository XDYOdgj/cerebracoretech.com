Elegant Stacked Slate Water Fountain

Bring tranquility and refined style to your outdoor space with this stunning Aquascape fountain. The stacked slate design gives it a natural, timeless appeal that effortlessly complements any garden, patio, or landscape. Whether you're looking to create a calming atmosphere or enhance your outdoor water fountain decor, this fountain is the perfect choice.

With a gentle, relaxing water flow, it's designed to create a serene ambiance and provide the soothing sound of water. The fiber resin composite construction ensures durability without the heavy weight of real stone, making it an ideal option for small garden fountain setups. It's also built to fit into compact spaces while delivering a striking visual impact.

This fountain works seamlessly with the AquaBasin 30 reservoir and the Ultra 800 pump (both sold separately), which helps maintain optimal water circulation and performance. It's a versatile addition to your outdoor environment, whether you're landscaping a large garden or adding a focal point to your backyard.
