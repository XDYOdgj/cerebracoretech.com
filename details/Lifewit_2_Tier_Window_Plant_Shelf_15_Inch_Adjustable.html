Lifewit 2-Tier Window Plant Shelf

Transform your window into a lush indoor garden with this stylish and functional 2-tier plant shelf. Designed with durable acrylic, it offers a sleek, transparent look that allows natural light to nourish your plants while adding a modern touch to your room's decor.

Each tier is adjustable with screws, letting you customize the height to suit your plants' needs. Whether you're growing succulents, herbs, or small pots, this shelf ensures optimal growth and neat organization. The secure threaded lock and included hex key are built to keep the shelves stable, reducing the risk of wobbling or tipping.

This space-saving design is perfect for small windowsills, and its versatile structure can also store kitchen tools, toys, or other household items. A great choice for anyone looking to maximize their indoor space with both utility and elegance.
