Lifewit Acrylic Floating Shelves

Transform your space with these sleek, modern window shelves. Designed to blend seamlessly with your window, they add a touch of greenery and style without cluttering your floor. The transparent construction lets your plants shine, making them an ideal choice for those who love to display their indoor gardens in a minimalist and elegant way.

Built with a powerful suction cup system, these shelves securely hold up to 10 pounds of weight, perfect for small plants, herbs, or even seed starter pots. The 1-inch front lip and side guards keep your items safe and prevent them from slipping or tipping over, giving you peace of mind with every arrangement.

With no drilling required, installation is a breeze. These shelves are not only functional but also a great way to add personality to any room, whether it's your kitchen, bathroom, or living area. A space-saving solution for plant lovers, they offer both style and practicality in one.
