Modern Brass-Tone Metal Umbrella Stand

Elevate your home's style and functionality with this sleek, contemporary umbrella stand. Designed for both indoor and outdoor use, it adds a refined touch to your entryway, mudroom, or foyer while keeping your essentials organized and protected from water damage.

Featuring a geometric shape and a polished brass-toned finish, this stand is perfect for those who appreciate modern aesthetics. The 20-inch height makes it ideal for storing larger umbrellas, walking sticks, or canes, and the built-in shelf accommodates smaller folding umbrellas with ease.
