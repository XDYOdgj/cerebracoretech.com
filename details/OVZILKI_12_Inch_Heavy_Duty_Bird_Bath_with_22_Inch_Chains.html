OVZILKI 12-Inch Heavy-Duty Bird Bath

Enhance your backyard birding experience with this durable and rust-proof bird bath, built to withstand the elements and provide lasting value. Featuring 22-inch chains that offer a secure and stable hanging solution, it's easy to install whether you're tying it to a tree branch or placing it on the ground. The 12-inch wide bowl is designed for multiple birds to gather, making it a great spot for both drinking and bathing. Whether you're looking to attract songbirds, hummingbirds, or a variety of feathered visitors, this bird bath delivers comfort, durability, and style all in one.
