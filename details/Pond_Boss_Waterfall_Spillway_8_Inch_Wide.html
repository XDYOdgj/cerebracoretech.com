Pond Boss Waterfall Spillway

Transform your outdoor space into a serene water paradise with the Pond Boss Waterfall Spillway. This sleek and efficient accessory is perfect for creating a natural, flowing waterfall or a peaceful disappearing stream. With a built-in water baffle, it ensures a gentle and steady flow of water, delivering a tranquil and visually appealing display.

Constructed from high-quality, UV-resistant plastic, the spillway is designed to stand up to the elements and last for years without fading or breaking. It's compatible with pumps ranging from 800 to 2500 GPH, making it a versatile choice for both pondless water features and traditional water gardens. The flat, symmetrical design allows for effortless leveling, ensuring a seamless installation process.

Beyond its durability, this spillway offers a clean and modern look that can easily blend with your landscape. Whether you're using landscaping slate or natural stone to hide it, its discreet profile makes it the perfect addition to any water garden setup. Ideal for homeowners and garden enthusiasts looking to enhance the aesthetics and functionality of their outdoor water features.
