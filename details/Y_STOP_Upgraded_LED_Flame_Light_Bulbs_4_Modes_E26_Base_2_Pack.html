Y STOP Upgraded LED Flame Light Bulbs

Key Features
  • 4 Modes: Experience flame simulation, breathing effect, general illumination, and gravity sensing for dynamic lighting effects.
  • Upside Down Flame Effect: Simulates the natural motion of fire rising from the bottom to the top for a realistic look.
  • E26 Base Compatibility: Easily install these bulbs into any standard fixture or decorative piece.
  • Heat Dissipation Design: Ensures longer lifespan and brighter performance for consistent lighting quality.

Whether you're looking for Halloween decoration bulbs, Christmas light bulbs, or simply want to add a romantic flair to your living room, these LED flames are a versatile and modern choice.
